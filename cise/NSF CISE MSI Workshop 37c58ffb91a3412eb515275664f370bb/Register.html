Registration & Travel

The University of North Texas (UNT) is hosting the NSF CISE MSI Aspiring PI Workshop at Denton Convention Center, Denton, Texas on Dec 12-13, 2024.

If you would like to attend please complete this registration form.

Sample Department Support Letter Template.

Notifications will be sent as to whether selected for participation on October 12, 2024. Participants will receive a stipend to cover or defray the cost of travel, meals, and lodging.

After selected for participation, you will receive instructions via email about how to arrange for travel and reimbursement for expenses. Travelers flying in should arrive at the Dallas DFW Airport.

The workshop will be held at a location near University of North Texas.
